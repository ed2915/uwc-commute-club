Privacy and consent

UWC Commute Club collects your student number, direction, suburb, and selected travel time only to group route/time interest.

Connection requests are saved for the organiser to review separately. They do not automatically share your details with anyone.

Your student number, or a student email address based on it, will not be shared with anyone in a pool unless you give explicit consent at a later stage.

Public route tables show aggregate counts only. Admin access is token-protected.

Request contact only works after your student number has already been added to this exact route/time group.

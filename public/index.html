Privacy and consent

UWC Commute Club collects your student or staff number, direction, suburb, and selected travel time only to record pool interest.

Connection requests are saved for the organiser to review separately. They do not automatically share your details with anyone.

Your student or staff number, or a UWC email address based on it, will not be shared with anyone in a pool unless you give explicit consent at a later stage.

Public pool tables show aggregate counts only. Admin access is token-protected.

Request contact only works after your student or staff number has already been added to this exact pool.

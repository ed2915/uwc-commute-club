Privacy and consent

UWC Commute Club collects your student number, direction, suburb, and selected travel times only to group route/time interest.

Providing your student number is voluntary, but it is needed to join route pools and avoid duplicate entries. Your route interest will be kept only for this commute-club project and should be deleted when it is no longer needed for that purpose.

You can remove selected route/time interests linked to your student number at any time using the remove button below. When a selected interest is removed, that route/time entry is deleted from the active database, and no extra copies are kept by this app that would continue to store it.

Your student number, or a student email address based on it, will not be shared with anyone in a pool unless you give explicit consent at a later stage.

Connection requests are saved for the organiser to review separately. They do not automatically share your details with anyone.

Public route tables show aggregate counts only. Admin access is token-protected.

To remove selected route/time interests, enter your student number, choose the same direction, suburb, and times above, then use the remove button.
